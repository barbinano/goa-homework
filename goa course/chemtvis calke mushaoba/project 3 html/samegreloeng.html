About Samegrelo

Samegrelo is a historical region in western Georgia, known for its unique culture, hospitable people, and unforgettable nature. You will find ancient monuments, traditional Megrelian villages, and a rich heritage. It is a place where modern life and traditions coexist in harmony.

Places to Visit

Martvili

Beautiful canyons and waterfalls. A popular tourist destination in western Georgia, known for its stunning nature and historical heritage.

Lebarde

Lebarde is a mountainous area in western Georgia, known for its fresh air, springs, and peaceful nature. It is especially attractive to those who want to relax away from the city noise.

Zugdidi

Zugdidi is one of the main cities of western Georgia, known for its historical museum, Dadiani Palace, and role as a cultural center of Samegrelo.

Hotels & Restaurants

Taler Terraces

Taler Terraces is a natural area near Martvili, famous for its beautiful views, green environment, and calm atmosphere.

Restaurant Samegrelo

A popular place in Zugdidi — if you want to try traditional Megrelian dishes like satsivi, elarji, or Megrelian khachapuri, this is a great option. The interior is cozy and modern, perfect for dinner with friends or family.
