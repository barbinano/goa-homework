About Imereti

Imereti is a historical region in western Georgia, known for its natural richness, caves, waterfalls, and ancient cultural monuments. Kutaisi — the regional center — holds significant cultural and historical importance. Bagrati Cathedral, Gelati Monastery, Prometheus Cave and Sataplia Reserve make Imereti one of the most important tourist destinations in the country.

Places to Visit

Bagrati Cathedral

11th-century cathedral considered a symbol of Kutaisi and part of Georgia's cultural heritage.

Prometheus Cave

A cave known for its unique stalagmites and stalactites, one of the most popular attractions in Georgia.

Sataplia

A nature reserve famous for its dinosaur footprints and a stunning glass viewing platform.
